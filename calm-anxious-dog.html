How to Calm an Anxious Dog Before and During Grooming

Grooming can be a stressful experience for some dogs. Here are some tips to help calm your anxious dog before and during grooming:

  • Start with short grooming sessions to get your dog used to the process.
  • Use calming aids like music or aromatherapy.
  • Provide treats and positive reinforcement.
  • Ensure the grooming environment is quiet and comfortable.
