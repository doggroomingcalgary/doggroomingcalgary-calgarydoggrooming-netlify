Grooming for Allergies: Tips for People with Pet Allergies

For individuals with pet allergies, grooming can be a challenging task. Here are some tips to help manage allergies while grooming:

  • Wear protective clothing and gloves.
  • Use hypoallergenic grooming products.
  • Keep the grooming area well-ventilated.
  • Wash hands and face after grooming.
