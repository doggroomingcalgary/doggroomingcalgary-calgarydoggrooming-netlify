How Often Should You Groom Your Dog? A Breed-by-Breed Guide

Grooming frequency can vary greatly depending on your dog's breed. Here are some guidelines to help you determine how often you should groom your dog:

  • Short-haired breeds: Groom every 4-6 weeks.
  • Long-haired breeds: Groom every 6-8 weeks.
  • Curly-haired breeds: Groom every 4-6 weeks.
  • Double-coated breeds: Groom every 8-12 weeks.
